Sausageroll

Home

Description

A flaky puff pastry wrapped around seasoned sausage meat, baked to perfection. Crisp outside, juicy inside and a classic bakery favorite.

ingredients

  • Puff pastry sheets
  • 250g sausage meat
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tbsp breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp paprika or nutmeg
  • 1 egg beaten

Steps

  1. Mix sausage meat wit onion, breadcrumbs and spices.
  2. Roll out puff pastry, cut into rectangles.
  3. Place sausages filling in center, fold pastry over, seal edges.
  4. Brush with beaten egg
  5. Bake a 400 degrees fahrenheit for 20-25 minutes until golden and crisp.
