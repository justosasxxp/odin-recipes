Description

lasagna is a classic italian dish made with wide, flat sheets of pasta layered with rich tomato sauce, seasoned meat(often beef or sausage), creamy ricotta or bechamel and plenty of melted cheese such as mozarella and parmesan. Baked until golden and bubbling, it delivers a heartly, comforting flavor with every bite

ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or italian sausage
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Salt & Black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Make meat sauce
  3. Prepare cheese mix
  4. Layer in a baking dish
  5. Top it off
  6. Bake
  7. Rest and serve
